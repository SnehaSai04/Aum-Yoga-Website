Yoganasa Book Of World Record

We am thrilled to share the pinnacle of our yoga journey. Our center achieved a remarkable milestone recognized by the Yoga Asana Book of World Records. We set a new world record by completing an astounding 216 rounds of Suryanamaskaras, comprising 2592 Asanas, in just 88 minutes. What makes this achievement even more special is that we spelled out the name of each asana during the practice. This has earned us the prestigious honor of being appointed as the head of Yogasana Book of World Records for Andhra Pradesh by the council.
Ramesh Sir is immensely grateful for this honor and excited for the journey ahead as we continue to inspire others on their yoga path. See More.
